Templates - Click to reveal/hide

Not happy with the prices on offer? No need to worry, that's what Templates are for! Any changes made to the price of materials can be saved as a Template for future use. Simply change a few prices and then enter a name for your Template and press 'Save Template'. On future visits to the site you can load your saved Template via the list at the bottom.

A few example Templates have been provided to give you an idea of how the system works. Simply select one and press Load Template. Don't worry if you delete them by mistake. refresh the page and the example Templates will return.
